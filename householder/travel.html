If you want to get a taxi directly from the airport or Edinburgh city centre, you are advised to contact the Hydro directly or negotiate with a local taxi firm. For example, Campbell's private hire of Peebles (tel./fax +44 1721 720664, email ian.campbell2@which.net) quotes £30 for a taxi for 1 to 4 people from the Edinburgh airport. Other local taxi firms are Standard Taxis (tel. +44 1721 721098) and Tweed Taxis (tel. +44 1721 722222).

There is a regular bus service (no. 62) from Edinburgh to Peebles. On a Sunday, buses depart hourly from Waterloo Place (just next to the train station, click here for a map) from 10.20am until 10.20pm, and the bus takes about an hour to get to Peebles. A single fare is around £4. The return weekday sevice is half hourly until 3pm then hourly until 10pm. If you alight at Peebles post office the hotel will be about 500m away (check this map, the arrow marks the post office) although if you ask, the driver may be able to drop you off even closer. A full timetable for this service should be available from the operator, First Edinburgh. As it doesn't seem to be available at the moment, an extract of the timetable can be found here.

Getting to Edinburgh

By rail. There is no train station in Peebles: the nearest main-line station is Edinburgh Waverley. Information on train timetables, fares etc. throughout Britain can be found here.

By air. The nearest airport to Peebles is Edinburgh International Airport. Direct flights are available from a number of European destinations (e.g., Amsterdam, Copenhagen, Frankfurt, London, Paris, Rome and Toulouse). There is a regular bus service from the airport into the city (costing around £4). A taxi ride will cost about £15.

Glasgow International Airport can be reached directly from a number of cities (including Chicago, New York and Toronto). From there, you can take a bus or taxi into Glasgow city centre, then a bus or train to Edinburgh. For example, a bus (from Buchannan Street Bus Station in Glasgow) will cost around £7 single trip and take 1hr 50mins, a train (from Queen Street Station in Glasgow) will cost around £8 single trip and take 50 minutes. There is no direct public transport link between Glasgow and Peebles. A taxi to Peebles will cost around £80.

By road. Detailed directions are available from the hotel website. For information on car hire you could try the following UK companies: BCR, National, Hertz, Thrifty.
