Householder Symposium XV, 2002

Accommodation: Preliminary Information

The accommodation package on offer covers the cost of five nights' accommodation and all meals (including the conference banquet) from dinner on Sunday, June 16th to lunch on Friday, June 21st, inclusive. We intend that the cost of this package (in pounds sterling) will be

  • £410.00 for a single room;
  • £260.00 (per person) for a shared room.
These figures represent a reduction of the hotel's actual rates by £200 per person: we are able to offer this deal thanks to sponsorship from EPSRC. Please note that these reduced rates will only apply to applicants whose abstracts are received before the 11th December 2001 deadline.

There is no registration fee for the meeting.

People who require further help with covering accommodation costs, for example, PhD students or other people with limited access to sources of funding, will be able to apply to the organisers for an additional subsidy in due course (after abstracts have been accepted by the committee).

We anticipate offering a room-mate finding service nearer the time for those who wish to share.

The rate for accompanying non-delegates will be £62.00 per person per day (for dinner, bed and breakfast). The rates for children (per day) are

  • 0 - 2 years £ 7.00
  • 3 - 6 years £14.00
  • 7 - 12 years £19.00
  • 13- 16 years £25.75
These rates entitle the children to high tea (i.e. early dinner), bed and breakfast. The hotel has a small number of family rooms, which consist of a room for the adults with a small room off the main room with bunk beds for the children.

The rate for dinner, bed and breakfast on extra nights before/after the meeting will be £62.00 per person per day.

All of the rates quoted include full use of most of the hotel leisure facilities.
